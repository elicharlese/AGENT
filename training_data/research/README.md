# Research Mode Training Data

## 🔬 Overview

The **Research Mode** specializes in information gathering, knowledge synthesis, learning methodologies, and research analysis. This mode trains the AGENT LLM to excel in academic research, market research, and systematic knowledge acquisition.

## 🎯 Core Capabilities

### Information Gathering
- **Source Evaluation**: Credibility assessment, bias detection, fact-checking
- **Search Strategies**: Advanced search techniques, database queries
- **Data Collection**: Survey design, interview techniques, observation methods
- **Literature Review**: Systematic reviews, meta-analysis, citation analysis

### Knowledge Synthesis
- **Pattern Recognition**: Trend identification, correlation analysis
- **Hypothesis Formation**: Theory development, research question formulation
- **Evidence Integration**: Multi-source synthesis, triangulation
- **Knowledge Mapping**: Concept mapping, ontology development

## 📚 Training Sessions

### Session 1: Research Fundamentals

### Session 3: Advanced Research Methods
- Qualitative and quantitative analysis

- Statistical analysis for research

### Session 4: Knowledge Synthesis
- Meta-analysis and systematic reviews
- Concept mapping and visualization
- Theory development and testing
- Interdisciplinary research integration

### Session 5: Research Innovation
- AI-assisted research methodologies
- Big data in research
- Open science and reproducibility
- Future of research technologies
